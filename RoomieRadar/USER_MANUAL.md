# RoomieRadar - User Manual

## 🎯 Getting Started

### What is RoomieRadar?
RoomieRadar is a platform that helps students find compatible roommates and book accommodation within their educational institutions. Our smart matching system analyzes your preferences to find the perfect living companions.

### Who Can Use RoomieRadar?
- Students with institutional email addresses
- Anyone looking for roommates in educational settings
- Students seeking accommodation in hostels or shared rooms

## 📝 Account Creation

### Step 1: Registration
1. Visit the RoomieRadar homepage
2. Click "Create Account" or "Get Started"
3. Fill in your details:
   - Full Name
   - Email (must be institutional email)
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Confirm Password
4. Click "Register"

### Step 2: Email Verification
1. Check your email inbox
2. Look for "Welcome to Roomie Radar - Activate Your Account"
3. Click the "Activate Account" button
4. Your account is now active!

### Step 3: First Login
1. Return to the homepage
2. Click "Sign In"
3. Enter your email and password
4. Click "Login"

## 👤 Profile Setup

### Complete Your Profile
1. After logging in, go to "Profile"
2. Add your information:
   - Profile Picture
   - Phone Number
   - Age and Gender
   - Academic Information
3. Click "Save Profile"

### Set Your Preferences
1. Navigate to "Preferences"
2. Configure your living preferences:
   - **Room Sharing**: How many roommates you want
   - **Cleanliness Level**: How clean you keep your space
   - **Study Habits**: When and how you study
   - **Sleep Schedule**: Your bedtime preferences
   - **Social Preferences**: How often you have guests
   - **Lifestyle Choices**: Smoking, drinking, food preferences
3. Click "Save Preferences"

## 🔍 Finding Roommates

### Smart Matching
1. Go to "Find Matches" from your dashboard
2. View your compatibility scores with other students
3. Each match shows:
   - Compatibility percentage
   - Basic profile information
   - Shared preferences
   - Room details (if they've already booked)

### Understanding Compatibility Scores
- **90-100%**: Excellent match - very similar preferences
- **80-89%**: Great match - mostly compatible
- **70-79%**: Good match - some differences but workable
- **60-69%**: Fair match - consider discussing differences
- **Below 60%**: Not recommended - significant differences

### What to Do If No Matches Found
If you don't see any roommate matches, the system will suggest:
- Empty rooms where you can be the first occupant
- Hostel-only rooms as alternative accommodation

## 💬 Messaging System

### Starting a Conversation
1. From your matches, click on a potential roommate
2. Click "Start Chat" or "Message"
3. Introduce yourself and discuss:
   - Living arrangements
   - Shared responsibilities
   - Common interests
   - Any concerns or questions

### Chat Features
- Real-time messaging
- Message read receipts
- Emoji support
- File sharing capabilities

### Safety Tips
- Keep conversations respectful
- Don't share personal information too quickly
- Report inappropriate behavior
- Meet in public places if arranging in-person meetings

## 🏠 Room Booking

### Browsing Available Rooms
1. Navigate to "Browse Rooms" or "Hostel Rooms"
2. Use filters to narrow your search:
   - Gender preference
   - Room sharing (1-4 beds)
   - AC/Non-AC
   - Budget range

### Room Information
Each room listing shows:
- Room number and type
- Total beds and availability
- Gender specification
- AC/Non-AC status
- Current occupancy
- Booking button

### Making a Booking
1. Click on your preferred room
2. Review room details
3. Click "Book This Room"
4. Confirm your booking
5. You'll receive a confirmation message

### Booking Restrictions
- Each user can only book ONE bed
- Once booked, you cannot book additional rooms
- Cancellations may be possible (contact support)

## 📞 Getting Help

### Common Issues

**Q: I'm not receiving the activation email**
A: Check your spam folder, ensure you used your institutional email, wait a few minutes for delivery

**Q: I can't find any matches**
A: Try adjusting your preferences, ensure your profile is complete, check if there are users in your area

**Q: Someone is bothering me**
A: Use the block and report features, contact support if needed

**Q: I want to change my booking**
A: Contact support as booking changes require manual processing
